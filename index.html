About Us

We Value Your Insights

E2M Statistics & Research Services is a data analytics and research consultancy based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. We convert raw data into practical evidence for decision making — across all project sizes and sectors.

From master's and PhD projects to donor-funded programmes and national studies, we bring advanced methods, clear communication, and ethical practice to every assignment.

Our multidisciplinary team covers biostatistics, econometrics, public health, and data science — providing the right level of support for your unique context.
