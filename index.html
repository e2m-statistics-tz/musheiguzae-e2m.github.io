About E2M Statistics

E2M Statistics & Research Services is a specialist statistical consulting firm offering comprehensive data analytics and research solutions tailored to the unique needs of businesses, academic institutions, health programs, and policy organisations in Tanzania and internationally.

Our consultants combine rigorous quantitative expertise with practical insight, ensuring every analysis is reproducible, methodologically sound, and communicated in language clients can act upon.

Technical Expertise

Statistical Modelling Capabilities

We deploy the right model for your data — choosing from a comprehensive toolkit of 11+ advanced techniques across cross-sectional, longitudinal, spatial, and Bayesian frameworks.

01

Linear & Generalised Linear Models

OLS, logistic, Poisson, negative binomial regression for diverse outcome types.

02

Time Series Analysis

ARIMA, SARIMA, VAR, forecasting & trend decomposition for temporal data.

03

Multilevel / Hierarchical Models

Mixed-effects models for nested and clustered data structures in health & education.

04

Structural Equation Modelling

CB-SEM and PLS-SEM for latent constructs, mediation, and moderation analysis.

05

Survival & Event History Analysis

Kaplan-Meier, Cox proportional hazards, parametric survival models.

06

Spatial & Spatio-Temporal Models

GIS-integrated analysis, spatial autocorrelation, kriging, disease mapping.

07

Bayesian Statistical Methods

MCMC estimation, Bayesian inference, prior elicitation for complex models.

08

Machine Learning Models

Random forest, gradient boosting, neural networks, cross-validation pipelines.

09

Panel & Longitudinal Data Models

Fixed effects, random effects, difference-in-differences, dynamic panels.

10

Meta-Analysis & Systematic Review

Fixed/random effects meta-analysis, heterogeneity, publication bias assessment.

11

Causal Inference Methods

Propensity score matching, instrumental variables, regression discontinuity.
