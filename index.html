Technical Expertise

Statistical Modelling Techniques

Our team applies state-of-the-art statistical methods to deliver rigorous, reproducible results across all research and analytics contexts.

01

Descriptive & Inferential Statistics

Summarizing data distributions, hypothesis testing, ANOVA, and comparative analysis.

02

Classical Regression Models

Linear, logistic, multinomial, ordinal, Poisson, and negative binomial regression.

03

Structural Equation Modelling

Path analysis, confirmatory factor analysis, mediation & moderation using SEM and SmartPLS.

04

Time Series Analysis & Forecasting

ARIMA, SARIMA, VAR, GARCH, and state-space models for temporal data.

05

Spatial & Spatio-Temporal Modelling

Geostatistics, disease mapping, INLA-based spatial models, and choropleth visualization.

06

Machine Learning & Predictive Analytics

Random Forest, XGBoost, ensemble methods, SHAP values, and deep learning applications.

07

Bayesian Statistical Inference

Posterior estimation, MCMC, INLA, hierarchical Bayesian models, and credible intervals.

08

Survival & Event History Analysis

Kaplan-Meier, Cox proportional hazards, accelerated failure time, and competing risks models.

09

Panel Data & Mixed-Effects Models

Fixed/random effects, GEE, multilevel models for longitudinal and clustered data.

10

Hierarchical Modelling

Multilevel nested structures, random slopes/intercepts, and cross-classified models.

11

Monitoring & Evaluation Analytics

Theory of change, LogFrame analysis, indicator tracking, and impact evaluation.
