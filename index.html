About Us

We Value Your Insights

E2M Statistics & Research Services is a data analytics and research consultancy based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. We help organisations and researchers of all sizes convert raw data into practical evidence for decision making.

From master's and PhD projects to donor-funded programmes and national studies, we bring advanced methods, clear communication, and ethical practice to every assignment.

Our team combines biostatistics, econometrics, public health, and data science — providing the right level of support whether your project is small, medium, or large.

Methods suitable for exploratory studies, operational monitoring, and high-impact research projects.

Descriptive & Inferential Statistics

Data summaries, hypothesis testing, ANOVA, comparative analysis.

02

Classical Regression Models

Linear, logistic, multinomial, ordinal, count models.

03

Structural Equation Modelling

Path analysis, CFA, mediation and moderation using SEM & SmartPLS.

04

Time Series Analysis & Forecasting

ARIMA, SARIMA, VAR, GARCH and state-space models.

05

Spatial & Spatio-Temporal Modelling

Machine Learning & Predictive Analytics

07

Bayesian Statistical Inference

Posterior estimation, MCMC, INLA, hierarchical Bayesian models.

08

Survival & Event History Analysis

Kaplan–Meier, Cox models, AFT models, competing risks.

09

Panel Data & Mixed-Effects Models

Fixed/random effects, GEE, multilevel models for longitudinal data.

10

Hierarchical Modelling

Nested and cross-classified multilevel models with random effects.

Monitoring & Evaluation Analytics

LogFrames, theory of change, indicator tracking, impact evaluation.
