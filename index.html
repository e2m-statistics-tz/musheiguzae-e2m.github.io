Technical Expertise Statistical Modelling Techniques Our team applies state-of-the-art statistical methods to deliver rigorous, reproducible results across all research and analytics contexts. 01 Descriptive & Inferential Statistics Summarizing data distributions, hypothesis testing, ANOVA, and comparative analysis. 02 Classical Regression Models Linear, logistic, multinomial, ordinal, Poisson, and negative binomial regression. 03 Structural Equation Modelling Path analysis, confirmatory factor analysis, mediation & moderation using SEM and SmartPLS. 04 Time Series Analysis &
