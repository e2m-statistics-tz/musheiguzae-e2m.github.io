About Us

We Value Your Insights

E2M Statistics & Research Services is a professional statistical consulting firm founded on the principle that rigorous data analysis transforms uncertainty into clarity and evidence into action.

We provide end-to-end support — from research design and data collection to advanced modelling, reporting, and capacity building. Our team brings internationally trained expertise to local and global research challenges, serving academia, government, health sectors, and development organisations.

We are committed to methodological rigour, reproducibility, and ethical practice in every engagement. Whether you are a PhD student, a policy analyst, or an institutional researcher, we deliver precision-driven insights tailored to your context.

Technical Expertise

Statistical Modelling Techniques

Our analytical repertoire spans 11 advanced modelling paradigms, enabling us to handle any research question with the appropriate statistical rigour.

01

Linear & Generalised Linear Models

OLS, WLS, GLM with various link functions; Poisson, Negative Binomial, Gamma regression

02

Logistic & Multinomial Regression

Binary, ordinal, and multinomial outcomes; ROC analysis and classification metrics

03

Survival & Time-to-Event Analysis

Kaplan-Meier, Cox proportional hazards, parametric survival models, competing risks

04

Mixed-Effects & Multilevel Models

Linear and non-linear mixed models; hierarchical / multilevel modelling for clustered data

05

Structural Equation Modelling (SEM)

Confirmatory factor analysis, path analysis, latent variable models, PLS-SEM

06

Time Series & Longitudinal Analysis

ARIMA, ARIMAX, panel data models, interrupted time series, growth curve modelling

07

Bayesian Statistical Methods

Bayesian inference, prior specification, MCMC sampling, probabilistic forecasting

08

Machine Learning & Predictive Analytics

Random forests, XGBoost, support vector machines, neural networks, model validation

09

Meta-Analysis & Systematic Review

Fixed-effects, random-effects, network meta-analysis; heterogeneity & publication bias assessment

10

Spatial & GIS Statistical Analysis

Spatial autocorrelation, kriging, disease mapping, geographic cluster detection

11

Hierarchical & Cluster Modelling

Cluster analysis, latent class analysis, hierarchical Bayes, mixed membership models
