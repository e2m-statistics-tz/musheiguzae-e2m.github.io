About Us

We Value Your Insights

E2M Statistics & Research Services is a professional data analytics and research consultancy headquartered in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. We transform complex data into actionable evidence that drives decisions — across all project sizes, sectors, and geographies.

From individual master's and PhD projects to large donor-funded programmes and national studies, our team brings rigorous advanced statistical methods, clear communication, and unwavering ethical practice to every engagement.

We specialise in Bayesian inference, spatio-temporal modelling, machine learning, evidence synthesis, and digital research solutions — building capacity and delivering results that matter.

Technical Expertise

Statistical Modelling Techniques

A comprehensive suite of methods — from classical inference to cutting-edge machine learning — applicable to exploratory studies, operational monitoring, and high-impact research.

01

Descriptive & Inferential Statistics

Data summaries, hypothesis testing, ANOVA, and comparative analysis.

02

Classical Regression Models

Linear, logistic, multinomial, ordinal, Poisson, and negative binomial regression.

03

Structural Equation Modelling

Path analysis, CFA, mediation & moderation via SEM and SmartPLS.

04

Time Series & Forecasting

ARIMA, SARIMA, VAR, GARCH, and state-space models for temporal data.

05

Spatial & Spatio-Temporal Modelling

Geostatistics, disease mapping, and INLA-based spatial/spatio-temporal models.

06

Machine Learning & Predictive Analytics

Random Forest, XGBoost, ensemble models, deep learning, and SHAP interpretation.

07

Bayesian Statistical Inference

Posterior estimation, MCMC, INLA, and hierarchical Bayesian models.

08

Survival & Event History Analysis

Kaplan–Meier, Cox PH, AFT models, and competing risk frameworks.

09

Panel Data & Mixed-Effects Models

Fixed/random effects, GEE, and multilevel models for longitudinal data.

10

Hierarchical Modelling

Nested and cross-classified multilevel models incorporating random effects.

11

M&E Analytics

LogFrames, theory of change, indicator tracking, and impact evaluation.
